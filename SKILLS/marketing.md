## Community Building

### Strategies
- Application-based (exclusivity drives value)
- Founder spotlights (recognition drives engagement)
- Regular events (consistency builds habit)
- Warm intros (utility drives retention)
- Content from community (ownership builds loyalty)

### Community Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Engaged members (30-day active) | > 40% |
| Intro completion rate | > 60% |
| Event attendance | > 50% of RSVPs |
| Content contribution | > 10% of members |

## Email Marketing

### Sequences

| Sequence | Trigger | Goal |
|----------|---------|------|
| Welcome | Approved | Complete profile, first connection |
| Re-engagement | 14d inactive | Return to platform |
| Event invite | New event | RSVP |
| Weekly digest | Every Sunday | Stay informed |
| Milestone | Achievement | Celebrate and share |

### Best Practices
- Bilingual subject lines (AR/EN)
- Mobile-optimized templates
- Personalized content based on founder stage
- Clear CTAs with single focus
