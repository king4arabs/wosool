# Marketing Skill

## Mission

Grow Wosool through trust, relevance, and ecosystem-aligned distribution rather than broad low-intent acquisition.

## Standards

| Focus | Standard |
|---|---|
| Messaging | Emphasize founder-to-founder value and credibility |
| Channels | Prefer partnerships, referrals, events, and high-context content |
| Analytics | Instrument before scaling spend |
| Regional relevance | Support Arabic and English communication where needed |
| Proof | Use real traction signals, not inflated claims |

## Current Priority

Prepare the platform for measurable growth by improving data flows, analytics readiness, and product trust indicators.
