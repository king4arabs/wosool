# AI Skill

## Mission

Prepare Wosool for safe, practical AI-assisted workflows without compromising trust, compliance, or product clarity.

## Standards

| Focus | Standard |
|---|---|
| Use cases | Prefer founder utility over gimmicks |
| Data handling | Avoid exposing sensitive content without explicit controls |
| Prompt design | Keep outputs auditable and role-appropriate |
| Governance | Document AI decisions and model dependencies |
| Rollout | Start with internal productivity and guided founder workflows |

## Current Priority

Delay heavy AI implementation until authentication, data quality, and analytics foundations are in place.
