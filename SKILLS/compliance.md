# Compliance Skill

## Mission

Keep Wosool aligned with the expectations of Saudi, GCC, and enterprise environments as the product matures.

## Standards

| Focus | Standard |
|---|---|
| Privacy | Prepare for PDPL-sensitive data handling |
| Transparency | Avoid overstating capabilities or endorsements |
| Localization | Support bilingual policy and communication needs |
| Auditability | Preserve decisions, releases, and operational changes |
| Vendor review | Evaluate third-party services for regional fit |

## Current Priority

Bring compliance concerns into architecture and operations early, especially around auth, data handling, and regional rollout.
