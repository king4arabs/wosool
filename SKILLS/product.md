# Product Skill

## Mission

Prioritize work that increases founder trust, execution speed, and regional relevance.

## Standards

| Focus | Standard |
|---|---|
| Prioritization | Choose the highest-leverage improvement first |
| Scope control | Avoid feature expansion without delivery readiness |
| Saudi-first relevance | Design for real founder and ecosystem workflows |
| Clarity | Keep product language precise and credible |
| Trust | Never overstate readiness or partnership status |

## Current Priority

Move from polished shells to real product utility through auth, live data, and operational trust.
