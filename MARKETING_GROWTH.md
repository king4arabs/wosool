# Marketing & Growth

> Wosool marketing strategy, growth channels, and go-to-market plan.

---

## Growth Strategy

### Phase 1 — Pre-Launch (Current)
- Build platform and content
- Establish brand presence (@AboutWosool)
- Create landing page with waitlist
- Network with early founder advocates
- Document ecosystem partnerships

### Phase 2 — Soft Launch
- Invite-only founder onboarding (50–100 founders)
- Partner co-launch events
- Content marketing (founder stories, ecosystem insights)
- Social media activation (Twitter/X, LinkedIn)
- PR in Saudi/GCC tech media

### Phase 3 — Public Launch
- Open applications with curated approval
- Event-driven growth (flagship Wosool events)
- Partner channel distribution
- SEO-optimized content hub
- Community-driven referrals

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

1. **Founder stories** — profiles and journeys of Saudi/GCC founders
2. **Ecosystem insights** — market analysis, funding trends, opportunities
3. **Execution guides** — practical advice on building, hiring, fundraising
4. **Community updates** — events, programs, milestones
5. **Partner spotlights** — partner profiles and collaboration highlights

### Content Calendar (Planned)

| Day | Content Type | Channel |
|-----|-------------|---------|
| Monday | Founder spotlight | LinkedIn, Twitter |
| Wednesday | Ecosystem insight | Blog, LinkedIn |
| Thursday | Event/program announcement | All channels |
| Sunday | Weekly founder roundup | Email newsletter |
