## Versioning Policy

Wosool follows **Semantic Versioning**.

| Change type | Meaning |
|---|---|
| MAJOR | Breaking product, API, or architecture changes |
| MINOR | Backward-compatible capability or delivery improvements |
| PATCH | Non-breaking fixes, corrections, and maintenance work |

## Current Release Rationale

Version `0.5.0` is a **minor release** that activates real member workflows on top of the v0.4.0 authentication foundation: members can now manage their founder profile and companies, RSVP to events, and apply to programs through dedicated `/api/v1/member/*` endpoints.
